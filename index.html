Yo Soy STEM

Title III HSI STEM Grant Program — San Diego City College
"Advancing the success of Latinx/Chicanx & low-income students in STEM"

✉ [email protected]

🌟 About the Program

"This competition specifically acknowledges the importance of student-centered programs that will increase the number of Hispanic and low-income students who graduate with degrees in STEM fields, as well as the need to promote support systems to ensure that community college students will continue to pursue STEM degrees once enrolled at a four-year institution." — U.S. Department of Education, 2021

Welcome! Yo Soy STEM is a federally-funded Title III HSI STEM Grant program right here at San Diego City College — and it was built for you. Whether you're just starting to explore science and technology, already know you want to transfer in a STEM field, or looking for real work experience while you study, Yo Soy STEM has free resources to support your journey.

Our program serves Hispanic and low-income students at SDCC by removing barriers, building community, and opening doors to STEM careers and university pathways. No matter where you're starting from — you belong here.

🛠️ Yo Soy STEM Makerspace

🔧 Yo Soy STEM Makerspace — Room R301

📍 3rd Floor, Learning Resource Center (LRC) — near elevator

Welcome to the Yo Soy STEM Makerspace, located near the elevator on the third floor of the Learning Resource Center (LRC) in room R301. This creative hub is open to all SDCC students, whether you're enrolled in a STEM program or not!

Bring your ideas to life through design, fabrication, and customization. No prior experience is necessary — Yo Soy STEM staff will show you how to use every piece of equipment.

📦 Materials provided at no cost for prototyping. Students creating items in bulk should bring their own supplies.

Available Equipment

✂️

Cricut Maker® 3

Drop-In AvailableNo Appointment

Cut over 100 different types of materials — vinyl, iron-on, paper, fabric, leather, and more. Perfect for stickers, apparel, and creative projects.

🗜️

Cricut Autopress™

Drop-In Available

A professional heat press for fabric and apparel. Transfer your Cricut designs onto T-shirts, tote bags, and wearables.

🔥

Glowforge Pro Laser Cutter/Engraver

📅 Appointment Only

Cut & engrave wood, cardboard, leather, and acrylic. Make signs, gifts, engineering prototypes. First visit = training session.

🖨️

3D Printer

Coming Soon📅 Appointment

Print 3D objects in plastic filament (PLA). Turn CAD designs into real physical prototypes for engineering, art, and more.

☕

Mug Press

Coming Soon

Press a custom vinyl design onto a mug. Perfect for personalized gifts, club merchandise, and keepsakes.

📋 Rules & Procedures

  • Drop-in Hours: Mon, Tue, Wed 2:00 pm – 5:00 pm; Thur 10:30 am – 2:00 pm (for machines that do not require appointments)
  • Appointments for Makerspace machines are for 1 hour. The Glowforge and 3D printer tend to take time, so appointments are required. The first appointment is always a training session on that machine.
  • Materials are provided at no cost for creating prototypes. Students creating items in bulk should bring their own supplies.

San Diego City College — Formally Designated Hispanic-Serving Institution

San Diego City College is proud to be formally designated a Hispanic-Serving Institution (HSI) by the United States Department of Education. This designation enables the Yo Soy STEM grant, which focuses on the intersection of HSI designation and STEM fields.

As an HSI, SDCC serves a student body that is majority Hispanic. SDCC is committed to student success, educational equity, and social justice — and Yo Soy STEM is one of the ways we live that commitment.
